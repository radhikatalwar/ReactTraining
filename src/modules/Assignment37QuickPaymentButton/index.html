What is Child Sponsorship? Child sponsorship is one of the best ways to give children better lives and futures. Sponsoring a child through World Vision India is one of the most effective, inspiring and rewarding ways to help children. You’ll be doing much more than helping to meet children's basic needs – you’ll be helping to equip them for life. By bringing about lasting change in your sponsored child’s community, you’ll also be improving the lives of many other vulnerable children. When you sponsor a child, you will receive photograph of your sponsored child, information about your child, child’s family and community, annual report indicating the progress of your child, World Vision India’s magazine Jeevan Sparsh and an opportunity to gift, write or visit your sponsored child.
